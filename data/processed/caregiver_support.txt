Caring for someone with dementia can be physically and emotionally challenging. Support services can help both the person with dementia and their caregivers.

Key strategies for dementia care include maintaining routines, creating a safe environment, encouraging physical activity, providing emotional support, and connecting with support groups and resources.

Family caregivers often experience stress, depression, and health problems. It's important for caregivers to take care of their own physical and emotional needs, seek respite care when needed, and connect with other caregivers for support and advice.
